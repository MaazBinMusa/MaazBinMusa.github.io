bio

I am currently a second year PhD student at the University of Iowa Department of Computer Science under the supervision of Dr. Rishab Nithyanand. Currently, I am a Research Assistant at Security, Privacy, and Anonymity Research Team (SPARTA Lab). I obtained my Bachelors from the National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (NUCES-FAST) Lahore in 2017, after which I worked at TPI (Technology for people initiative) Lab for a year working on media discourse and security.

research interests

My research interests are in the areas of security, privacy, and Internet measurement. More specifically, I enjoy research in information flow containing eco-systems such as measuring aspects of the advertising and tracking online, tracking data leakages from social media and misinformation discourse online.
